LAETUS. / Unity MLAgents

My exploration with MLAgents (Unity package allowing use of PPO training) began as a high school independent study into AI and Machine learning. My end goal of that semester was to have an agent be able to beat the first level of LAETUS.

This was a success and allowed me to take my first steps into training agents and designing environments. This portion of my website is dedicated to documenting my journey and progress in training AI through increasingly complex environments.

Origin.

This is footage of my agent running inference off of the model that I trained in May of 2023. Sadly, this is the only footage I took during that time as I didn't realize how useful documentation was. I took up this project again in January of 2024 basically starting from square one, as the orignal project has been filled with errors after accidentally being opened in a newer Unity version.

I have since decided to delve deeper into MLAgents and truly understand what is happening with every techinical decision. I plan to work through some simple levels to determine more effecient environments for training. After I work through the simple levels, I will go through the levels of LAETUS and each unique challenge each level presents.
