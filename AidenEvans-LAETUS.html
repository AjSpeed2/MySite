LAETUS.

LAETUS. is a precise 2D-Platformer that will challenge your mental fortitude and physical abilites. With simple design and gameplay mechanics, exploiting your colour-changing and movement abilites will bring you success. The developer really didn't hold back on these levels...

LAETUS. began as a summer hobby as I had spare time and was looking for something productive to do. I quickly learned that I had a passion for game development and ended up using my capstone as an excuse to follow my passion. I continue to work on LAETUS. to this day. You can check out the GitHub repository here and if you would like to try the game, send me an email and I can give you a key.
